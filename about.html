About

In Prichi, you traverse through randomized chessboard layouts as you take out every opposing chess piece that stands in your way! You start with just a Queen, but can find other pieces scattered around and add them to your party. The goal of each level is to eliminate every other chess piece on the board. Try and get through as many levels as you can!

Genre

Dungeon Crawler / Arcade / Turn-based Strategy

These 3 genres are my favorite genres to play, and so I tried to combine them into a game that was not only fun for me to create, but fun for others to play.

Platform

At the time of writing this, there is no deeper meaning or story. However, that might change as the game gets developed further.

Aesthetics

The game will have a pixel art style. Simple, and can have a really unique effect on the atmosphere for the game. I am very fond of pixel art styled games, and love to make my games with graphics like them.

Gameplay

I want to keep the gameplay of the game as simple as possible but still make it require some strategy and thought power.

When you start a level, a random level layout is created. Based on the level size, a certain amount of black chess pieces are placed randomly throughout. You then choose where to start your white chess pieces. At the start of the game, you only start with a Queen piece, but can find other white chess pieces and add them to your party as you go.

At the bottom of the screen, there will be a "turn order" of the opposing pieces. Only 1 of the pieces on either side (your party or the opposing party) can move each turn. The "turn order" at the bottom tells you which of the opposing pieces will move next.

To beat a level, you must capture all of the other chess pieces on the board. If you lose your queen piece, you lose. You can lose other pieces that you collect without losing, but you lose them for good after that (unless you find another one in a later level).
